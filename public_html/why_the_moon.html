Why the Moon?

The moon still has many unanswered questions. NASE will go to the moon to further research understanding the moon. On the expedition, we will try to search for inhabitants, explore the near and farside of the moon, and examine the terrain.

Moon Fun Facts

The moon is 238,857 miles away.

The diameter of the Moon is the same distance from New York City to Phoenix, Arizona.

The moon orbits the Earth every 27.3 days

Neil Armstrong was the first human to set foot on the moon.

There is no atmosphere on the moon.
